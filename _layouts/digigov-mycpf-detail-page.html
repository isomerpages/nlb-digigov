myCPF

Central Provident Fund

myCPF provides easy access to your personal CPF information and enables you to perform a variety of functions on the go using your SingPass.

What you can do with myCPF:

  • View a summary of the balances in your accounts
  • View statements for your healthcare, housing and investment schemes
  • View your transaction and contribution history up to 15 months before
  • Top up CPF savings using cash or CPF for yourself and your loved ones
  • Calculate your monthly CPF contributions payable and see their allocation
