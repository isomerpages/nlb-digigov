HealthHub

Ministry of Health

HealthHub offers a range of health and wellness related content, deals, rewards and digital services. Access yours and your family’s health records through this one-stop portal.

What you can do with HealthHub:

  • Make, view, reschedule and cancel medical appointments
  • Access prescription records and medication lists
  • Request for medication refills or renewal of prescriptions
  • Pay for medical bills
  • Track your child’s developmental milestones and vaccination, as well as upcoming immunisation schedules
  • View your parents’ medical appointments and medication lists
  • Get notified of school health assessments and your child’s referral letters
  • Find doctors, caregivers, 24-hr Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS) clinics easily
  • Receive Healthpoints when you read articles and complete quizzes
