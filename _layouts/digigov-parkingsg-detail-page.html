Parking.sg

Government Technology Agency

Parking.sg allows you to pay short-term parking charges through your mobile device at all coupon-based public car parks, as an alternative mode of payment to paper parking coupons.

What you can do with Parking.sg:

  • Calculate parking fees automatically by keying in your vehicle number, selecting the car park and indicating your estimated parking duration
  • Pay parking fees using credit or debit card
  • Track and extend parking sessions remotely, and receive notifications when your parking sessions are expiring or have ended
  • End parking sessions early and receive refunds based on the actual parked duration
