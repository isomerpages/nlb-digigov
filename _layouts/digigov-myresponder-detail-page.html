myResponder

Singapore Civil Defence Force

myResponder alerts members of the public to nearby fire and medical cases before ambulance arrival, to save lives and mitigate minor fires in the first few critical minutes.

What you can do with myResponder:

  • Receive geolocation notifications of nearby fires and cardiac arrest cases
  • View nearby cases using OneMap
  • Dial 995 with a geolocation-enabled function
  • Access the National Automated External Defibrillator (AED) Registry
  • Provide on-site information via submission of photos and videos
